Infographics for The Chronicle

Overview

From August 2015 to May 2017, I created infographics for The Chronicle, the student-run paper at Duke. I usually made these in about two hours, due to the deadlines of a daily paper.

At The Chronicle, I first served as an Associate Design Editor, and then as the Graphic Design Editor.

Tools: Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch 3
