Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, I have always had a passion for building and creating, whether it be paintings or Legos. When I entered college, I also developed interests in tech and psychology. Upon discovering the world of product design, I was immediately fascinated by the challenge of marrying desirability, feasibility, and viability in designing solutions.

In May 2019, I graduated from Duke University with a Computer Science major and a Psychology minor. Currently, I am designing construction solutions at BuildingConnected, an Autodesk company. Previously, I interned as a product designer at two other companies; check out my resume here.

My hobbies include photography, reading, and trying new baking recipes but often sometimes failing (I like to think I've improved). I also obssess over filmmaking and the show Mr. Robot.
