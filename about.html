Hi, my name is Carolyn! I am currently a senior at Duke University obtaining a major in Computer Science and a minor in Psychology. Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, I have had a passion in drawing and painting since I was a young child. Since entering college, I've also developed interests in tech and psychology, and now I work on projects relating to the intersection of those two fields and design — namely, UI/UX/Product design.

My hobbies include photography, reading, and trying new recipes but often sometimes failing (I like to think I've improved). I also obssess over cinematography and the show Mr. Robot.
